Save Nature #1

The Green Earth Club works to protect natural resources through tree planting, clean-up drives, and awareness programs. We encourage communities to adopt eco-friendly habits and reduce pollution for a sustainable future.

Eco Activities #2

Our initiatives include recycling workshops, beach clean-ups, rainwater harvesting programs, and climate education campaigns. Together, we aim to make our environment healthier and greener.
